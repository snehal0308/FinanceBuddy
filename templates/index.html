Voice-Activated Budget Management:
Users can manage their finances through voice commands, making it easy for elderly users to interact with the app without needing to navigate complex interfaces.
6 Jars Method Integration
The app automatically divides income into six predefined categories (Necessities, Financial Freedom, Education, Long-Term Savings, Play, and Give) to help users manage their finances more effectively.
Expense Tracking and Summaries:
Users can add, track, and categorize expenses by simply speaking to the bot. The app provides summaries and reports of their spending, making it easy to monitor financial health.
Reminders and Alerts:
The app can set up voice-activated reminders for bill payments and financial alerts, helping users stay on top of their financial obligations and avoid late fees or overdrafts.
